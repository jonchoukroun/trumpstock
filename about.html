Why?

Companies don’t typically take a political stance….but these aren’t typical times. Here at Twenty20, our employees do not have the same political views. We do not agree on how to handle the nuances of our fiscal, monetary and foreign policy - and that’s okay. However, we’re all concerned about what’s happening in America today.

Today, we’re concerned that there’s an attack on our American identity:

when we attempt to ban a specific religion from entering our country,

when the Japanese-American internment camps are quoted as a ‘precedent’ for immigrant registry by the president’s team,

when we ignore conflicts-of-interest by breaking from the presidential tradition of releasing tax returns,

when we ignore the decades-long attack on overreaching presidential powers,

when we dismiss intelligence & military officers,

when confusion becomes a political tactic,

when the press becomes the enemy,

and when facts become ‘alternative facts’,

Then there’s something wrong.

Look, we aren’t a political powerhouse with enormous reach -- we’re a twelve person startup with a dream to build a business and help our users. But, no matter how small our team, we feel an obligation to do something.

So, we’d like to introduce Trump Stock Photos
